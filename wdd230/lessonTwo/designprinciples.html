Design Principle #2: White Space & Clean Design

  • Example company: Mountain Project
  • link
  • It is imporant to have plenty of space between elements on your page, and I think Mountain Project does an excellent job with this. Though there may be a lot of information on a page, the spacing doesn't make it feel overwhelming.

Design Principle #3: Contrast

  • Example company: Evernote
  • link
  • Contrast is probably my favorite concept because it is the best thing to grap someone's attention. It is also neccessary (because without it, some information may become unreadable...and no one wants that). Evernote is a great example with its green-and-black coloring.
