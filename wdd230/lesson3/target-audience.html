This is the target-audience page

Target audience: local business owners
Persona 1: Tom Wood; 52 years of age; Has owned his hardware shop for nearly thrity years and hopes to pass it down to one of his four sons.
Persona 2: Jackie Lofthouse; 31 years of age; Has taken her first real steps into startting her own business, so that she can work from home.

Scenario 1: Tom recognizes that times are changing, especially in the ways of business. One of his sons has helped him set up a website, but
he doesn't really know what to do from there. He hears that the Chamber of Commerce is having an event that has a workshop in social media marketing. Tom
is interested, and looks for the place, date and time on the chamber of commerce website.
Scenario 2: Tom enjoyed the event, and decided to look on the chamber of commerce website for more things that could help him. He found that if he became a member,
there are a lot of benefits, like getting discounts from other local business like him. Sounds like a good deal!
Scenario 3: Tom has had many great things come from being a member of the chamber of commerce. So much so, that he has decided to give back. With his many years
of experience and new-found knowledge: he decided to go onto the chmber of commerce website and sign up to be a mentor.
Scenario 4: Jackie has made great strides in starting her business, but when it comes to book keeping: she is a little overwhelmed. The upcoming
tax season has her stressed, but luckily she found on the chamber of commerce website that there will be a free class on the subject. Sounds like a good idea!
Scenario 5: Jackie's business is starting to slow down. She is afraid that she may have to go back to a traditional job to pay the bills. Luckily, at her class,
she found out that there are people who volunteer to be business mentors, and that she can find their names on the chamber of commerce website.
