A little about Bountiful Food's history:

It all started with a young, poor college student named Joan Ann. She had a simple dream: to touch the lives of people as far as she could reach. She had lots of childhood memories of her and her mother making juices from fresh produce. This led to her making a little extra money from selling similar juices. The next thing she knew: her business grew. Now owning several trucks that operate from Carlsbad to LA California, she takes great pride in knowing she has helped so many local farmers.

Fun Fact about our CEO:

Have you ever wondered why our logo has an apple, rather than any other fruit? Even if you didn't, you'll be interested in knowing that Joan Ann's favoirite fruit out of all of them is the apple!
