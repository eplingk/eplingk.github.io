Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce

Just beyond our "Welcome" sign is our famous roundabout with mountain statue. On this statue displays many stone animals, including the wild cat and parent eagles searching for food for their young in a nest high above.

In front of one of our golfing parks, we have one of Peter Wolf Toth's famous "Whispering Giants". The project having started in 1972, Mr. Toth has made one of these magnificent pieces of art to place in every one the United States.

Idaho Falls Zoo at Tautphaus Park is one of the many places where you can enjoy a learning experience with your family. It is a beautiful location with many fun creatures.

The Greenbelt, circling the snake river, is a popular trail. It is perfect for walking, biking, or even spending some time admiring the beautiful river.

Next to the Greenbelt is our Idaho Falls Temple. A gorgeous structure with the angel Moroni proudly on top, playing his trump.
